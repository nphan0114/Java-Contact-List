    Become the point of contact and representative for LinkedIn GTS for all internal on-boarding at LinkedIn
    1. Listed below are the set of responsibilities that each technician in this sub-team is tasked to:
      1. On-boarding weekly New Hires
      2. Account Creation
      3. Inventory Management for New Hires
      4. Deployment
      5. Working and communicating with other internal LinkedIn team (HR/Facilites)
      6. Documentation

    Gather New Hire Data

    RT and JIRA tickets
    1. Check the New Hire queue in RT for FTE and JIRA for Contractors
    2. Perform extensive searches using start dates or full name
    3. Midweek new hires or late new hires will be completed at the end of the 3rd business day
  • +

    Gather New Hire Equipment

    It is your job to understand the basic standards and follow them unless it is escalated and is approved by Albert
    1. Utilize your Hardware Standards knowledge to determine what types of systems are going to be prepped and imaged (see link above).
    2. Department that receives Cisco Phone by default
      1. Finance
      2. Sales
      3. Recruiting
      4. Corp Communications
      5. Marketing
    3. Use your best judgement.
  • +

    Account Creation

    Account Management Tool (AMT)
    1. Once you've completed gathering all new hire data, check to see who the On-call Technician: go/dsoncall
    2. Submit ServiceNow ticket of Re-hire & Conversion for the On-call Technician to enable/update the accounts
    3. Please read guide on how to create account via AMT (see link above)
    4. Sao Paulo new hire - create account and notify Rafael Freire
  • +

    Provisioning Only (Outside Vendors)

    Contingent new hire that will not attend orientation
    1. Follow normal provisioning process.
    2. Follow 3-5 business days SLA
    3. Coordinate pick up location with hiring manager once system is ready.
  • +

    Remote home office

    Home Office new hire that requires laptop to be shipped
    1. Ship out only the laptop and charger and record the tracking info on go/raptops for respective user
    2. Plan shipment to arrive on legal start date.
    3. Do not ship out new hire login info sheet.
    4. Forward the new hire info sheet in .PDF to hiring manager.
  • +

    Email only requests

    "Account Only" new hire will not need I.T. equipment
    1. All requests must be sent to the Hiring Manager and include the new hire sheet in PDF Format only.
    2. Forward the new hire's ticket to hiring manager and attach PDF version on the NH sheet

    On-boarding EGC new hire

    EGC new hire requires special provisioning. See POC listed above.
    1. Work with DS-Oncall to create the account
    2. Have DS-Oncall add new hire to restricted groups for EGC
    3. Provision a X1 with base image
    4. Submit RITM for Peter Masa and request for EGC configuration and provide the following:
      1. hostname
